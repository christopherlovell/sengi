About

A speedy SPS spectra viewer, using Principal Component Analysis for reconstruction.

Sengi is being actively developed on github. If you find any bugs, or wish to make a feature request, please submit an issue.

Health Warning

Because of the nature of the PCA reconstruction the spectra generated by Sengi will not be exact. We show in the associated paper that the accuracy is within 5%, sufficient for prototyping and education purposes, however for science purposes the original models should always be used.
